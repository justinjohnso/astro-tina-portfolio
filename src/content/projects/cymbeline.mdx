Written by William Shakespeare around 1610, *Cymbeline* is a tale of royal intrigue, strange coincidences, romantic betrayal, war and divine guidance. The production team worked create a unique fable-like environment using a range period references (in the clothing and audio) stretching across the 20th and early 21st centuries.
