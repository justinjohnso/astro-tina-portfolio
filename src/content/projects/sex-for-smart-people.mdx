[**Sex For Smart People**](https://web.archive.org/web/20220122184741/http://www.sfsppodcast.com/) (That Means You!) is a podcast by Orion Johnstone and David McGee. They think that authenticity and communication are the sexiest. They were partners (like sexy partners!) for six years. While they were partners — and also now that they are not — they created their own vocabulary around their connection, in such a way that inspired friends and friends of friends and people they didn't even know come to them all the time for relationship and sex advice.

I was responsible for mixing the last 10 or so episodes.
