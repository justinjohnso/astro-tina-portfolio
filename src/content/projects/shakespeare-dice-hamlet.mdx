Seven actors learn the entire script of *Hamlet*. Every night, the audience rolls the dice to determine who plays who and the show goes on.

*‍Shakespeare Dice: Hamlet* was [Dacha Theatre](https://web.archive.org/web/20211207234558/http://www.dachatheatre.com/)'s second show in the Dice format. After a successful run of *Shakespeare Dice: Twelfth Night* last spring, we were excited to bring this theatrical feat to a tragedy. Audiences tested the odds and joined in our investigation of this haunting play, discovering with us what follows from the opening line: "Who's there?"
