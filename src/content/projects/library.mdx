**Content notice: sounds of gunshots & physical violence.**

This soundscape tells the story of a personal library, through all of the people who have existed in it, and all of the events that have happened there.This was the final project for my Sound Design class in 2014. The goal was to create a full, multi-channel story of a space, object or location using only sound.I built the soundscape using Logic Pro for editing and QLab to compile and play back the audio.
