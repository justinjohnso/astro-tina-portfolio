A location-based app that will let you chat with any other online users, as long as you are on a General Assembly campus.Written in Elixir, Phoenix backend.

This was part of a challenge to learn a new coding language over the weekend. I selected Elixir as I had only worked in-depth with JS and Ruby at that point, and was interested in how a functional programming language worked.
