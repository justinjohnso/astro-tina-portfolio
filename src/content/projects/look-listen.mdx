Lighting Design: Sam Whitney

Choreography: Sabina Moe
 
Look/Listen makes use of [wearable tech](https://www.plusea.at/?p=2388) to give the performers direct control over lights and sound, and plays with putting these design elements at the forefront of the story.

It follows the antics of two humans who stumble upon a sentient ethereal light and its otherworldly caretakers.

Through building the MIDI gloves, this project sparked my passion for combining technology with art.
