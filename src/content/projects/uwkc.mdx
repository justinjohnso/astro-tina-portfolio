I was a Web Developer and Content Strategist at United Way of King County in 2018. I helped maintain the** **[**main website**](https://www.uwkc.org/)** **and was responsible for designing and building pages for events and fundraising campaigns throughout the year.

I've linked some examples of projects I worked on while at United Way. They represent near-final designs, but may not be representative of version hosted on the company site.
All design comps are built with Webflow and hosted on Netlify.

[Style guide](https://uwkc-style-guide.netlify.app/) - Web-specific styling and brand guidelines, geared towards agency and freelancer use.

[Homepage refresh](https://uwkc-homepage-refresh.netlify.app/) - One of the design iterations for the updated homepage on the UWKC website.

["New to Town"](https://uwkc-new-to-town.netlify.app/) - Part of an outreach campaign for people new to Seattle.

["Day of Caring"](https://uwkc-day-of-caring.netlify.app/) - The event page for Day Of Caring, a city-wide day of volunteering organized by UWKC.

["Fundraise Online" - Version A](https://uwkc-fundraise-online-a.netlify.app/) - One option for UWKC's new "fundraise online" page.

["Fundraise Online" - Version B](https://uwkc-fundraise-online-b.netlify.app/) - A second option for UWKC's "fundraise online" page.

["Live United"](https://uwkc-live-united.netlify.app/) - Signup page for the "Live United" newsletter.
