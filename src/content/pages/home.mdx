# Welcome

I'm a creative technologist and sound designer building interactive experiences that explore the intersection of sound, space, and human interaction.

From motion-controlled instruments to immersive soundscapes, I create work that invites curiosity and play. Whether it's designing responsive audio for theatre, building tangible interfaces for music-making, or crafting sonic environments for dance, I work with generous constraints that help people engage with technology in embodied, expressive ways.

Browse my projects to see how I'm exploring these ideas through code, sensors, fabrication, and sound.
